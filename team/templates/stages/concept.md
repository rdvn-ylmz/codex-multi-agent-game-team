Objective:
Define the product concept and positioning for the target audience.

Inputs:
- User problem statement and target market assumptions.
- Current project constraints (time, budget, team size).

Outputs:
- 2-3 concept options with one recommended direction.
- Risks and validation plan for each option.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Recommended concept includes a clear USP.
- Includes measurable assumptions for validation.
- Provides handoff to game_design.

Handoff:
Pass concept rationale and assumptions to game_design.
